In the Philippines, mais con yelo is a traditional dessert of crushed ice layered with corn kernels and sweetened milk. For this version, the milk and ice are combined with corn to make a delicious granita..

Torta is 'omelet' and talong is 'eggplant' but this Filipino dish is more like an egg-battered cutlet eaten for breakfast or lunch.

For adobo that is sweet, salty, tangy, garlicky, and ready in a fraction of the time, don not peel and slice each garlic clove: Just cut open a whole head and simmer it in the sauce.
